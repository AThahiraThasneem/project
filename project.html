Cakes

Cake is a flour confection made from flour, sugar, and other ingredients and is usually baked.

Cake is often served as a celebratory dish on ceremonial occasions, such as weddings, anniversaries, and birthdays. There are countless cake recipes; some are bread-like, some are rich and elaborate, and many are centuries old.

Cookies

A cookie (American English), or a biscuit (British English), is a baked or cooked snack or dessert that is typically small, flat and sweet.

Cookies are produced according to varied formula compositions, in many different shapes and sizes, and by various manufacturing procedures.Cookies are popular as snacks or desserts and are often associated with a particular season or occasion.

Pastries

They are beloved for their flaky, tender, and buttery qualities

Pastries are a diverse category of baked goods that encompass a wide range of sweet and savory treats.

Pies and Cobblers

Pies and cobblers are classic and comforting desserts that have been enjoyed for generations.

They both feature sweet or savory fillings, but their toppings and preparation methods differentiate them.

Tarts

They are known for their buttery, flaky crusts and can be filled with sweet or savory ingredients.

Tarts are a versatile and delicious category of baked goods that come in various shapes, sizes, and flavors.

Brownies

Brownies are a popular dessert that is beloved by many people.

They are a type of baked treat that is often characterized by their rich, fudgy texture and chocolate flavor.
