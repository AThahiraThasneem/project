Ingredients:
-8 tablespoons unsalted butter, cut into pieces
-6 ounces semisweet chocolate, finely chopped
-1/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
-3 large eggs, at room temperature
-1 cup sugar
-1 teaspoon vanilla extract
-1 cup all-purpose flour
-1 cup dulce de leche
Instructions:
1.Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease an 8-inch square baking pan with nonstick cooking spray.
2.In a medium saucepan, melt the butter over low heat. Add the chocolate and stir until melted and smooth. Remove from heat and whisk in the cocoa powder until smooth.
3.In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the eggs, sugar, and vanilla extract until well combined. Add the chocolate mixture and whisk until smooth.
4.Add the flour and stir until just combined.
5.Pour half of the batter into the prepared pan. Drop spoonfuls of dulce de leche over the batter. Swirl the dulce de leche into the batter with a knife.
6.Pour the remaining batter over the dulce de leche layer. Drop spoonfuls of dulce de leche over the top of the batter. Swirl the dulce de leche into the batter with a knife.
7.Bake for 30-35 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out with a few moist crumbs attached.
8.Let the brownies cool in the pan for 10 minutes, then transfer to a wire rack to cool completel
y. 9.Cut into squares and serve.
