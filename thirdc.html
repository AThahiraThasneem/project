Ingredients:

-4 cups all-purpose flour
-1/3 cup granulated sugar
-4 teaspoons active dry yeast
-2 1/4 teaspoons kosher salt
-1 1/4 cups unsalted butter, cold
-1 cup milk, cold
-1 large egg, beaten with a teaspoon or two of water

Instructions:

1.In a medium bowl, mix together the flour, sugar, yeast, and salt.
2.Cut the cold butter into small pieces and mix it into the flour mixture until it resembles coarse crumbs.
3.Add the cold milk and mix until a dough forms.
4.Knead the dough on a floured surface for 5-10 minutes, until it is smooth and elastic.
5.Wrap the dough in plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.
6.Roll the dough out into a large rectangle and fold it into thirds like a letter.
7.Turn the dough 90 degrees and roll it out again into a large rectangle.
8. Fold the dough into thirds again and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.
9. Repeat steps 6-8 two more times, for a total of four folds.
10. Roll the dough out into a large rectangle and cut it into triangles.
11.Roll each triangle up into a croissant shape.
12. Place the croissants on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
13. Brush the croissants with the beaten egg.
14. Let the croissants rise for 1-2 hours, until they are puffy.
15. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
16. Bake the croissants for 15-20 minutes, or until they are golden brown.
17.Cool for 5 minutes before removing to wire racks to cool completely.
Enjoy your delicious homemade croissants!
