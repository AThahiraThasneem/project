Recipe for White Forest Cake:

Ingredients:

- 2 ½ cups all-purpose flour
- 2 cups sugar
- 1 cup milk
- ½ cup unsalted butter, softened
- ½ cup vegetable oil
- 4 large eggs
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 1 teaspoon almond extract
- 1 cup cherry pie filling
- 1 ½ cups heavy cream
- ½ cup powdered sugar
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 1 cup white chocolate shavings

Instructions:

1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease and flour two 9-inch round cake pans.
2. In a large bowl, cream together the softened butter and sugar until light and fluffy.
3. Add in the eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition. Stir in the vanilla and almond extract.
4. In a separate bowl, whisk together the flour and baking powder.
5. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the butter mixture, alternating with the milk. Begin and end with the flour mixture, mixing until just combined. Do not overmix.
6. Divide the batter evenly between the prepared cake pans, smoothing the tops with a spatula.
7. Bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
8. Remove the cakes from the oven and let them cool in the pans for 10 minutes. Then transfer them to a wire rack to cool completely.
9. Once the cakes have cooled, place one layer on a serving plate. Spread a generous amount of cherry pie filling evenly over the top.
10. In a separate bowl, whip the heavy cream until soft peaks form. Gradually add the powdered sugar and vanilla extract, continuing to whip until stiff peaks form.
11. Spread a layer of whipped cream over the cherry pie filling on the first cake layer. Place the second cake layer on top.
12. Frost the top and sides of the cake with the remaining whipped cream.
13. Decorate the cake with white chocolate shavings, gently pressing them onto the sides and sprinkling some on top.
14. Refrigerate the White Forest Cake for at least 2 hours before serving to allow the flavors to meld and the frosting to set.
15. Slice and serve the delicious White Forest Cake to enjoy the luscious combination of flavors.

Note: You can also add additional cherries or cherry syrup between the cake layers for extra cherry flavor.
