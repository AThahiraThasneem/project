Ingredients:
2 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon baking soda
1/2 teaspoon baking powder
1 cup unsalted butter, softened
1 1/2 cups white sugar
1 egg
2 teaspoons vanilla extract
Instructions:
1.Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
2.In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, baking soda, and baking powder.
3.In a large bowl, beat the butter, white sugar, and vanilla extract until creamy, about 2 minutes.
4.Add the egg and beat well.
5.Gradually beat in the flour mixture.
6.Roll the dough into 1-inch balls and place them on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper
. 7.Flatten the balls with a glass or measuring cup.
8.Bake for 8-10 minutes, or until the edges are lightly golden.
9.Cool for 5 minutes before removing to wire racks to cool completely.
10.Enjoy your delicious homemade sugar cookies!
