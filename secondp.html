Ingredients:
-1 cup peanut butter
-1 cup white sugar
-1 large egg
Instructions:
1.Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
2.In a medium bowl, mix together the peanut butter, white sugar, and egg until smooth
. 3.Roll the dough into 1-inch balls and place them on an ungreased baking sheet.
4.Flatten the balls with a fork, making a criss-cross pattern on top.
5.Bake for 8-10 minutes, or until the edges are lightly golden.
6.Cool for 5 minutes before removing to wire racks to cool completely.
7.Enjoy your delicious homemade peanut butter cookies!
