Here's a recipe for making a delicious chocolate tart with a chocolate ganache filling:
**Ingredients:**
**For the Tart Crust:**
- 1 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
- 1/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
- 1/2 cup (1 stick) unsalted butter, cold and cubed
- 1/4 cup granulated sugar
- 1 large egg yolk
- 2 tablespoons ice-cold water
**For the Chocolate Ganache Filling:**
- 8 ounces (about 1 1/3 cups) semisweet or bittersweet chocolate, finely chopped
- 1 cup heavy cream
- 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
- 1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
**Instructions:**
**1. Prepare the Tart Crust:**
a. In a food processor, combine the flour, unsweetened cocoa powder, and granulated sugar. Pulse a few times to mix.
b. Add the cold, cubed butter and pulse until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
c. In a small bowl, whisk together the egg yolk and ice-cold water. Add this to the food processor and pulse until the dough comes together. If needed, add a little more water.
d. Turn the dough out onto a lightly floured surface and shape it into a disk. Wrap it in plastic wrap and chill in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes.
**2. Preheat the Oven:**
a. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
**3. Roll Out and Shape the Tart Crust:**
a. On a floured surface, roll out the chilled dough into a circle, about 12 inches in diameter.
b. Carefully transfer the rolled-out dough to a 9 or 10-inch tart pan with a removable bottom. Press the dough into the pan and trim any excess. Prick the bottom of the crust with a fork to prevent it from puffing up during baking.
**4. Bake the Crust:**
a. Line the tart crust with parchment paper and fill it with pie weights, dried beans, or rice.
b. Bake in the preheated oven for about 15 minutes. Remove the weights and parchment paper and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes, or until the crust is set and slightly dry. Let it cool completely.
**5. Prepare the Chocolate Ganache Filling:**
a. Place the finely chopped chocolate in a heatproof bowl.
b. In a saucepan, heat the heavy cream and butter until it just starts to boil. Pour the hot cream mixture over the chopped chocolate and let it sit for a minute.
c. Stir the chocolate and cream together until the mixture is smooth and the chocolate is completely melted. Stir in the vanilla extract.
**6. Fill the Tart:**
a. Pour the chocolate ganache into the cooled tart crust and spread it evenly.
**7. Chill:**
a. Place the tart in the refrigerator and let it chill for at least 2-3 hours, or until the ganache is set.
**8. Serve:**
a. Slice and serve your chocolate tart. It's rich and luxurious, making it a perfect dessert for special occasions.
You can garnish your chocolate tart with whipped cream, fresh berries, or a dusting of cocoa powder for an extra touch of elegance. Enjoy your homemade chocolate tart!
